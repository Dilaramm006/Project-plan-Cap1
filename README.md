3. **Project Title**

Online Food Ordering System

---

4. **Problem Statement (Why is this system needed?)**

Many restaurants still take orders manually by phone or in person. This process can be slow, confusing, and may cause mistakes in orders. Customers also need to spend time waiting or traveling to the restaurant.

An Online Food Ordering System allows customers to easily browse menus, select food, and place orders through the internet. This system helps restaurants manage orders more efficiently and improves customer satisfaction.

---

5. **Project Objectives**

The objectives of this project are:

- To design a simple and effective online food ordering system.
- To allow customers to view food menus and prices.
- To allow customers to place food orders online.
- To help restaurant staff manage incoming orders.
- To improve ordering speed and reduce mistakes.

---

6. **Scope of the System**

The system will include the following features:

- Customer registration and login
- Viewing restaurant menu
- Selecting food items
- Adding items to cart
- Placing orders
- Staff viewing and managing orders
- Manager monitoring system activity

The system will **not include real payment integration or delivery tracking** in this project.

---

7. **Expected Users**

The main users of the system are:

- **Customer** – browse menu and place orders
- **Staff / Employee** – manage and prepare orders
- **Manager** – monitor orders and system activity

10. **Conclusion**

The Online Food Ordering System helps restaurants improve their service and manage orders more effectively. Customers can order food quickly and easily without visiting the restaurant. This project demonstrates how system analysis and design can be used to solve real-world problems and improve business processes.
